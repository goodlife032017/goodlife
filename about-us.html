About Good Life

Good Life was established in March 2017 and has its headquarters in BF Homes Paranaque. Our community provides healthcare services, personal care programs, senior care management, and more.

Mission

Good Life mission is to offer new beginnings and to have meaningful life to elders while providing home care services built on innovation, skills and CHRIST like values of compassion, patience, honestly and love not far away from home.

Vision

Good Life vision is to be the elderly care provider of choice that will asssist seniors and the challenged to realize the quality of lifethat recognizes their independence supported by caring services, respect, trust and love.
